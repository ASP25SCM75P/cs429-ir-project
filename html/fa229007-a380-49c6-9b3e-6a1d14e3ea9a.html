Bag of Words Model

The bag-of-words model is a simplifying representation used in natural language processing and information retrieval. In this model, a text is represented as the bag (multiset) of its words, disregarding grammar and even word order but keeping multiplicity. The bag-of-words model is commonly used in methods of document classification where the occurrence of each word is used as a feature for training a classifier. An example of the bag-of-words model is used in spam filtering. The model has been used in computer vision as well. The bag-of-words model is one of the foundational concepts in information retrieval and forms the basis for many modern search and classification techniques.
